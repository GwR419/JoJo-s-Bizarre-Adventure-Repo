Jean Pierre Polnareff

Chapter 127

The TERROR brought about by this ARROW...

Which I obtained after the battle in egypt 12 years ago...

Will not end unless THEY come here!

It must be THEM.

I cannot do that myself.

Only those who know the TRUE POWER of this ARROW can surpass KING CRIMSON and its RULE OVER TIME.

They will become my hope...

Once they meet this arrow,

I'd have to destroy the arrow.
